About pinitto.me

PinItTo.me is an infinite virtual corkboard. Collaborate with friends, family, or colleagues in real time in any way you want. Make plans, share ideas, play games...

This is a free service (please donate to help cover costs) and the code will be open sourced very soon. Please feel free to make suggestions for improvements and let us know about any of those nasty bugs.
